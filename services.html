Choose which servers store your data

GoldFynch.com stores data on cloud servers based in North America, but GoldFynch Services gives you other options.

Store data on your own server

  • We install GoldFynch on a local server on your office premises. You use your own equipment – based on our specifications – or buy equipment directly from us (and we take care of warranties and maintenance).

  • You get complete control of content: Files are stored on your own devices, move over your own networks, and are processed on your own server.

  • However, it’s the most complicated and expensive option . You need to have a capable IT team, handle your own security and network administration, maintain your own equipment (if you haven’t bought it from us), and handle data backup, software upgrades, etc.

Contact us for a detailed quote or an on-premises solution>>

Price: $20,000 and above, depending on the options you select.

Store data on a private server

  • We run GoldFynch on a ‘leased’ server dedicated solely for you. The server is still on a cloud farm hosted by a third party, but is exclusively yours. Data is encrypted and transmitted from your office to the server for processing and storage.

  • You get all the advantages of cloud computing. You don’t have to worry about server maintenance, upgrades, and backups; and you will also have access to the server from anywhere.

  • This costs less that the on-premises option above, but more than the regular GoldFynch.com cloud option.

Contact us if you want a dedicated server>>

Store data on a server-cloud from your continent

  • We find you a cloud-provider on your continent. The servers used to store your data will be subject to the laws of your region.

  • You get all the advantages of cloud computing . You’ll automatically get our latest software updates and benefit from our proactive error handling. Plus, we handle maintenance.

  • The prices will be far less than ‘on-premises’ option , but higher than our North American GoldFynch.com prices (since cloud services outside USA tend to cost more).
