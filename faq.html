What is GoldFynch?

GoldFynch is a cloud-based, e-discovery review platform provided as “software as a service”.

GoldFynch functions much like other web-based software you use, like email: all you have to do is go to the website (goldfynch.com) and create an account for free. If you already have an account log in to start using it. Once you are logged in, just create a case. You can then drag and drop files (PDF, emails, Word docs etc) into GoldFynch. The files are automatically processed and made instantly searchable. It’s like having your own private search engine.

What is E-discovery?

e-Discovery (or electronic discovery) is a broad term used to describe collecting documents in an electronic format (such as PDF, Microsoft Word etc.) and searching for information within the documents. It also includes the production or export of documents in an electronic format.

Sometimes paper documents are scanned and the text is electronically extracted by OCR to make electronic documents. This is also e-discovery. The length and breadth of e-discovery is well described in this Wikipedia entry.

Is E-Discovery only for lawyers & law firms?

The Short Answer: NO!

The Long Answer: e-Discovery is a term from the legal industry that reflects the ever-increasing amount of data involved in preparing for a case (potentially millions of documents in varying file types need to be reviewed for each case, including emails, memos, bank accounts, spreadsheets, diagrams, etc). We’ve included some industry-specific features for the legal field (such as a super-fast Bates Number search and upload alerts for missing Bates Numbers), but the majority of GoldFynchs’ e-Discovery features are universal.

The reality is that everyone can benefit from e-Discovery. Whether you’re an academic, business owner, student, or just overwhelmed by any collection of documents or datasets, GoldFynch offers e-Discovery, simplified.

Features and Functionality

What software & hardware do I need?

An Internet connection and a browser on your laptop, desktop, phone, or tablet is all you need to use GoldFynch.

We recommend Chrome, Firefox, or Safari browsers for the best experience. GoldFynch runs on the cloud and is software as service, so you don’t need any dedicated hardware or software.

What is a case?

A case is the container for your data.

You can create as many cases as you like. All searches, activity, tags etc., are based only on the data uploaded to a specific case. You can create different cases for each issue or dataset on which you are currently working.

    Security

    Where is my data being uploaded?

    Your data is being uploaded to a secure location in the cloud.

    Only you can access your own data. For both the drag and drop browser option and the dedicated uploader, your data is encrypted during transmission to our site.

    Is my data safe?

    Just like your clients value the security and confidentiality you provide, we take security very seriously.

    Your data is always safe with us.

    We use industry standard, state of the art encryption technology. Your data is encrypted during transmission to our cloud sites and stored behind firewalls – the same security levels used in online banking.

    What happens when I delete a case?

    All the data within the case is completely erased from our system.

    We do not back up deleted cases. We have safeguards in place to ensure you don’t accidentally delete a case.
